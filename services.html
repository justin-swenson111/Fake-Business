Purpose

Our purpose is to bring improved air quality to your home, all in the size of a normal plastic water bottle! Here in O'Hare Air, we made air both affordable and accessible to all members of our community.

Our founder, Aloysius O'Hare, is very passionate about the wellfare of the people, big and small, young and old. And when he saw the high dangers of polution, he knew something had to be done fast. And that was the push towards the founding of the company. We started small, literally speaking. We started with baby bottles, soda cans, any container we could think of, until we began to think big and got to the different sizes and services we have today. Now, Mr. O'Hare does everything we can, and is beginning to expand to other parts of the world aboard his blimp.
